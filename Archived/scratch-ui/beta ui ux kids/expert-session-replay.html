Dr. Klein · Entomologist · Nature

Session has ended. All answered questions are below.

Maya asked
What would happen if bees disappeared?
Dr. Klein

About a third of the food we eat depends on bees pollinating flowers. Without them, a lot of fruit, nuts, and vegetables would become rare and much more expensive.

Reese asked
Do bees sleep in the hive at night?
Dr. Klein

Yes — they rest in shifts, so the hive is never totally "off." Worker bees take short naps, sometimes with their heads tucked into a honeycomb cell.

Nina asked
Are there bees that aren't yellow?
Dr. Klein

Lots. There are blue orchid bees, green metallic bees, and even completely black carpenter bees. Over 20,000 species exist worldwide.
