Treasury Suspends Enforcement of 145% China Tariff for 90 Days

The Treasury Department announced a 90-day suspension of enforcement actions on the 145% tariff applied to Chinese imports since March 2, citing ongoing bilateral negotiations in Geneva. The pause does not reduce the tariff rate and expires July 19. Beijing's Ministry of Commerce called the move "insufficient but noted."
